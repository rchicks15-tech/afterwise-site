Communication volume between the Government PM office and Contractor XYZ increased 34% in the 18 days following Mod 7 (engineering requirement change). This spike correlated with a 12-day delay on CDRLs A004 and A006, contributing to a 3.1% schedule slip and an estimated $214K cost overrun. Recommendation: Establish a formal change notification protocol requiring joint impact assessments within 5 business days of any contract modification.
Production milestone MS-4 was missed by 23 days. Root cause trace: 6 of 9 predecessor tasks were delayed by an average of 4.2 days due to unresolved RFIs — 83% of which originated from a single subcontractor. Recommendation: Require weekly RFI burn-down reporting from all Tier-1 subcontractors; escalate any RFI unresolved beyond 7 days to the COR.
Cost Performance Index (CPI) dropped from 0.97 to 0.84 between months 8–11, driven by a 17% labor hour overrun in systems integration. Engineering change orders in this period averaged 2.3x the baseline estimate. Recommendation: Apply a minimum 1.4x labor multiplier to ECO estimates for integration-phase work in future contract planning.
